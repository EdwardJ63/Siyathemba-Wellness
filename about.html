Welcome to Styathernba Wellness Center

Who We Are

Empowering Communities Through Education & Change

At Styathernba Wellness Center, we work to combat pressing community challenges such as alcohol and drug abuse, gender-based violence, and the spread of HIV/AIDS through education and awareness.

Our Mission

Transforming Lives Through Awareness & Education

We aim to create holistic solutions through advocacy, outreach programs, and fostering positive behavioral change in communities affected by substance abuse, violence, and health crises.

Our Core Values

Guiding Every Step We Take

  • Compassion — Advocating for those in need.
  • Integrity — Building trust through transparency.
  • Innovation — Driving progress through creativity.
  • Collaboration — Strengthening community partnerships.
  • Education — Empowering individuals through knowledge.
