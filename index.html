HIV/AIDS

  • Knowledge is Power—Get Tested, Stay Informed!
  • Over 38 million people worldwide live with HIV/AIDS.
  • Early detection and treatment can significantly improve life expectancy.
  • Modern treatments allow people with HIV to live long, healthy lives.
  • Education reduces stigma and discrimination.

Alcohol & Drug Abuse

  • Choose Life, Not Addiction.
  • Substance abuse can lead to long-term health issues.
  • Addiction often causes social isolation.
